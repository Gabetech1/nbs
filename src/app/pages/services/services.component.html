Ship Management
Technical ship management is the core of our business. Our ship management services include all aspects of managing vessels both operationally and technically.
Crew Management & Training
The Nulbulk group recruits and trains officers and ratings through our manning centres in Canada, Latvia, Philippines and Russia.
Technical Services
Through the expertise of our naval architects, deck and engine superintendents we offer a wide range of technical services globally.
Quality Assurance
Our quality and safety systems are central to our business; fundamental to safeguarding all areas of environmental protection and life at sea and the basis of a harmonious and efficient operation. We aim to continually develop all aspects of our quality and safety standards in order to comply with international regulations and improve our efficiency and performance for our clients.
Crew Travel
Baltic Marine Travel SIA currently services the crew travel requirement of the Nulbulk managed fleet. With full IATA accreditation and agreements with all major airlines to issue marine fares, Baltic Marine Travel are able to obtain the most competitive airfares.
