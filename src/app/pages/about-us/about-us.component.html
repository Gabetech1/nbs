Who is Nulbulk

Nulbulk Shipping was established 35 years ago to provide technical ship management, crewing and technical services to ship owners. To this day we remain committed to continuously providing our clients with consistently high standards of operations and services. Although our core business activity is technical ship management, we also have significant experience in a variety of related areas.

The present managed fleet consists of; bulk carriers, chemical and oil tankers, container ships, gas carriers and reefer ships with our management offices located in Glasgow and Riga as well as satellite offices in Manila, St. John NB, St. Petersburg and Kaliningrad.

In an increasingly complex and regulatory industry we maintain our competitive edge through a proactive approach; by investing in people at sea and ashore to equip them with the skill and knowledge needed to further their careers and improve marine operations.

Mission

Our purpose is to provide our ship-owner clients with the highest levels of services on a consistent basis thereby enhancing our mutual reputations and profitability.

Our Values

Commitment and enthusiasm as demonstrated through our work and actions. Building long term relationships through teamwork with our colleagues, both shore based and at sea, and with our clients, suppliers and partners. We want these relationships to be based on trust, honesty, integrity and respecting people as individuals.

Risk Awareness and Management

We seek to avoid unacceptable risks to people and the environment as well as plant, equipment and capital. We aim to protect life and the environment.

Innovation

We recognise that the world around is always changing. We want to learn about new techniques, technologies and opportunities to improve our company and our industry.

Profitability

We aim for a satisfactory return on capital in order to have the resources to invest for our future and ensure business continuity.
